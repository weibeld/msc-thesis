 JFLAP
-----
Java Formal Languages and Automata Package. Intendd as a pedagogical tool.
Graphical tool with different functionality on three levels:
1) Regular languages: DFAs, NFAs, regular grammars, regular expressions, conversions (e.g. NFA->DFA, NFA->regex, NFA->regular grammar), equivalence testing, running automaton on input words (step by step, traces, etc.)
2) Context-free languages:pushdown automata, context-free grammars, conversions (e.g. pushdown automaton->context-free grammar, context-free grammar->pushdown automaton)
3) Recursively enumerable languages: turing machines, unrestricted grammars
http://www.jflap.org/


GOAL
----
Graphical Tool for Omega-Automata and Logics.
Allows various operations on LTL formulas and their corresponding omega-automata.
Omega-automata: creating automata with B端chi, Muller, Rabin, Streett, and Paritiy acceptance conditions, input testing, equivalence testing, conversions between the different omega types, determinization (different procedures), complementation (different procedures)
LTL formulas: translate to different omega automata types.
Built-in access to the B端chi Store
http://goal.im.ntu.edu.tw/wiki/doku.php

FAT
---
Finite Automaton Tool
